About PICT

About Us

Pune Institute of Computer Technology (PICT), established in 1983, is a premier engineering college in Pune, Maharashtra, India. Affiliated with Savitribai Phule Pune University, PICT is renowned for its excellence in computer and information technology education.

Our Mission

To provide quality education in engineering and technology, fostering innovation and research to meet global challenges.

Our Vision

To be a leading institute in technical education, producing skilled professionals and contributing to societal development.
